How to play

Guess the hidden athlete. Every guess gets a heat emoji and a similarity rank. Lower rank means closer. The list always sorts closest to coldest.

Emoji guide: wrong sport is cold, right sport is 🔥, and right division/team is 🔥🔥.

Player pool: all listed players are guessable, but daily answers are chosen from more recognizable names.

Ranking: same sport, team, division/league, position, nationality, and age all help determine how close your guess is.

Daily mode: everyone gets the same daily athlete. Practice mode gives you a random one.

Scoring: solve faster, use fewer guesses, and avoid clues for a higher score. Revealing the answer gives only 250 points.
