How To Play

Try to guess the hidden sports player by typing any NFL, MLB, NBA, or Premier League player you think is related.

Rank (#) shows how close your guess is compared to all other players. Lower is better.

Match details: each guess shows Sport, Conf, Div, Team, and Pos. A ✓ means that detail matches the secret player.

Examples:

Secret Player: LeBron James
Luka Doncic → #6 (very close)
Patrick Mahomes → #344 (close-ish)
Erling Haaland → #1,100 (far)

Your most recent guess appears in the gray row at the top, and the full list below stays sorted by closest rank.

Use clues if you need help, but clues lower your score.

Community stats count players only after they submit at least one guess.
